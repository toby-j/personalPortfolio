About me

I'm Toby, a computer science undergraduate from Surrey University.

I have a passion for music and creating things that help solve the world's biggest issues: artificial intelligence and climate change.

I've played guitar since I was 16 and founded a band at my first year of university. Recently, I've picked up DJing and am looking to start the first DJ society at my University.

When I was 18, I founded my first business, BePanda. With sustainability at the forefront, I sourced and marketed recycleable, reuseable products on Amazon.co.uk and at a number of garden centres.

My latest project, BeautyTruth, aims to accelerate the transition to sustainable and ethical products.

Technologies I'm using to make my projects happen

Quickly know if a product is vegan, cruelty- free or sustainable. All from your smartphone.

Since July 2020, I've been developing BeautyTruth natively for IOS and Android with an aim to let consumers know quickly which vegan and cruelty-free societies have certified a scanned product.

I've designed an intricate system using AWS, Azure and external APIs to connect my app to over 34 million products. This system will correctly deal with new and phased out products while increasing the speed of frequent product scans over time.

I've partnered with PETA to have exclusive use of their API. I'm also in discussion with other societies to negotiate access to their certified products and brands to help streamline development.
